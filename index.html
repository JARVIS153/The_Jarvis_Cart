About The Jarvis Cart

Empowering college students with quality products that make campus life better, easier, and more stylish


Our Story

Founded by Hemant Chand, The Jarvis Cart was born from a simple idea: college life should be affordable, convenient, and full of style. We understand the unique needs of students and curate products that perfectly blend quality, affordability, and trendiness.

"Our mission is to transform the college shopping experience, making it accessible, reliable, and enjoyable for every student across India."

- Hemant Chand, CEO
